About

Zani's Plant Based Cakes was born from a passion for creating beautiful, delicious desserts that are accessible and kind to animals and the earth.

A Sweet Journey

My journey into vegan baking started with family. My sister has severe allergies to milk and eggs, and I wanted to create sweet treats she could enjoy without worry. As I experimented with plant-based ingredients, I discovered that delicious desserts didn't need animal products at all. Over time, I learned more about the ethical concerns and environmental impact of animal agriculture. That knowledge led me to embrace veganismâ€”not just in my baking, but in my life. I continued to refine my recipes, proving that plant-based cakes can be just as rich, moist, and flavorful as their traditional counterparts, all while reducing harm to animals and the planet.

Today, my goal is simple: to share these delicious, thoughtfully made cakes with everyone. Whether you're vegan, have dietary restrictions, or just love a good dessert, I'm here to make sure you never have to compromise on taste.
